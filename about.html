About Aqua Clean

Aqua Clean is a community-powered platform created to help people raise their voice against water pollution and sanitation problems. Whether it’s contamination from the Saradha river or unsafe drinking water in rural areas, our goal is to empower you to report and track these issues easily.

The system allows citizens to submit complaints about unclean water sources, broken pipelines, or other related issues. These complaints are stored securely and shared with concerned officials to ensure fast and responsible action.

"Clean water is the world's first and foremost medicine." – Slovak Proverb

We follow the vision of United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6 (Clean Water & Sanitation). Through this website, students and citizens are working hand in hand to make sure clean water becomes a reality for all.
