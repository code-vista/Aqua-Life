About Aqua Clean

Aqua Clean is a mission-driven platform committed to ensuring clean and safe drinking water access for every citizen. Through community engagement and digital tools, our goal is to tackle water-related issues, starting with the Saradha river contamination crisis.

We provide a space for users to register complaints about water pollution, infrastructure breakdowns, and sanitation problems. This data is shared with local authorities, ensuring your voice reaches the right place.

Clean water should not be a luxury, but a right available to everyone.

With our easy-to-use web portal, citizens can file complaints, monitor progress, and read educational content. Aqua Clean aligns with UN SDG 6: Clean Water & Sanitation, and we work closely with students and civic bodies to bring real change.
